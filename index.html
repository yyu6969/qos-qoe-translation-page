QoS-QoE Translation

A source-grounded dataset of structured QoS-QoE relationships from the multimedia literature.

Paper Dataset Code Supplementary Material

Overview

QoS-QoE Translation is a dataset of structured QoS-QoE relationships extracted from the multimedia literature, with a current focus on video streaming.

  • 505 curated papers
  • 1026 source-grounded relationship records
  • JSON-based structured format
  • Source-grounded evidence and contextual metadata

Abstract

We introduce QoS-QoE Translation, a source-grounded dataset of structured QoS-QoE relationships from the multimedia literature. The dataset is designed to support benchmarking, modeling, retrieval, and LLM-based reasoning for multimedia quality prediction and optimization.
